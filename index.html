Running on an off-chain ledger 
Inter-operable with Ethereum Main Net

To address cost and scalability accompanied with micro transactions, L0yalX runs on an off-chain ledger operated by its tokens holders, earning tokens for running nodes, while maintaining interoperability with main ethereum network where you can transfer your tokens and redeem them for Ethers or any other ERC20 tokens.
